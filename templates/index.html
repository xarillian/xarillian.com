I'm Austin Heinrich. I call myself a "techno-optimist." We have saved ourselves with machines.

Originally from the edge of the Canadian Shield, I moved at 18 for university to Saskatoon, Canada. I've been in the silicon prairie ever since.

As a teenager, I used to say, "one day, I'll learn to code," while I was already building games with ruby-on-rails. I didn't realize I was actually a software developer until I had a degree in my hand. Since that moment, I've been excited to work on games, user experience, and AI.

When I'm not online, I'm at the pool or crafting stories. Swimming is a quick entry into the flow state; nothing is better than a morning spent in the water. Likewise, nothing is better than a night in front of a glowing monitor.

I live with my beautiful wife, Erika, and our three cats. They keep me grounded.
